## What to build

Add time-based Gothic Pack Earning to the compact Expedition View. The player should see Next Pack progress fill over time, ready pack count increase when progress completes, and a Pack Ready Signal appear on the Pack Navigation Button without forcing a Pack Opening Sequence.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Gothic Pack Earning progresses over time without requiring dungeon completion.
- [ ] Next Pack progress is visible in the compact overlay.
- [ ] Completing progress creates a ready Gothic Pack.
- [ ] Ready pack count is represented in the UI.
- [ ] A Pack Ready Signal appears on the Pack Navigation Button when at least one pack is ready.
- [ ] The Pack Ready Signal does not interrupt Expedition Watching or auto-open a pack.
- [ ] Pack earning behavior is covered by thin tests for progress, ready count, and capped or full state.
